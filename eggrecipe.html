Scrambled Eggs

here is a recipe for scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
id= "method">

method

  1. melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter had melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the eggs from the edg of the pan every twenty seconds
  5. when the eggs are still moist removed from the heat
